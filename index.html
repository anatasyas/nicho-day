Happy 23rd Birthday, Nicholas

Another year, another chapter, another reason to be grateful. Aku mau ngucapin selamat, bukan cuma untuk ultahnya aja, tapi untuk everything we've been through together this past year. Terima kasih yaa sayang, for continuously showing me what love really means—the kind that grows deeper, quieter, more real.

Selamat menikmati another year of adulting kak nicholas hehe. But just a gentle reminder ya, i got your back, always. Dalam setiap keputusan, setiap langkah, setiap moment of doubt—aku ada di sini. Aku bakal terus ada di samping kamu, supporting every dream you chase and every goal you set.

Kalau aku inget-inget lagi, di umur 22 kemarin banyak banget yang terjadi ya—kamu graduated, diterima kerja sebagai contract, dan yang paling special... we made it official. Ketiga hal itu somehow jadi milestone yang aku personally appreciate banget, karena aku berkesempatan untuk witness dan jadi bagian dari semua momen penting itu.

Satu tahun udah berlalu sejak ultah kamu yang ke-22, dan somehow aku masih amazed at how you keep making me fall deeper. Kamu nggak cuma jadi partner, tapi juga best friend, support system, dan home yang aku always wanna come back to.

Thank you for being patient with me, for understanding me in ways that sometimes even i don't understand myself. Thank you for staying, for fighting with me (and sometimes against me haha), and for making this relationship feel worth every single effort.

Jangan bosen sama aku ya sayang, because i still have sooo many plans for us! Masih banyak tempat yang mau aku explore bareng kamu, masih banyak late night talks yang belum kita obrolin, masih banyak memories yang pengen aku bikin sama kamu.

May your 23rd year be filled with growth, peace, and all the happiness you deserve. May we continue to build something beautiful together, despite everything and everyone.

All my love, always ♡
